# Questions 

1. What is the expansion of $(a+b)^2$? 

2. What is the expansion of $(a-b)^2$? 

3. Factorise: $a^2-b^2$ 

4. Solve for $x$: $x^2+5x+6=0$ 

5. Let  $h[n] = (0.4)^nu[n]$. Find the value of
\begin{equation*}
\sum_{n=0}^\infty |h[n]|^2
\end{equation*} 

6. Find the roots of $f(x) = 2 x^2 + 3 x + 4$.
